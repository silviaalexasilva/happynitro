Norway Ice City

Is it a sunset? Or sunrise? Why not both!
The majority of Norway (its northern part) is above the Arctic circle and every summer the sun literally never sets! This phenomenon is called the midnight sun and during summer months the sun is above the horizon for 24 hours. The midnight sun is characteristic for all Scandinavian countries but it lasts the longest in Norway.

Cherry in Japan

A hidden botanical delight in the heart of the atmospheric city of Kurayoshi!
You can wonder and see the spring cherry blossom blooms.

Bali Aww Gate

Handara Iconic Gate is the path way to serenity!
The traditional Hindu gate symbolises the entrance from the outer world to the temple, or in this case, golf course, and plays an important role in Indonesian culture. Pamper your eyes with the amazing green surrounding, beautiful scenery, and the amazing background of smoky green hills between the gates that creates a perfect picture for your holiday.
Go and soak for a few hours in the natural volcanic hot springs at Sekigane-onsen.
